Welcome Message

Welcome to Crowe International Schools, we are a private Nursery, Primary and Secondary School located in Iwo, Osun State of Nigeria. Over the years we have continued to facilitate the development of young minds and our academic success is recognised nationally. We are a place where students thrive knowing that they are loved, here they develop a thirst for learning and a sincere love for God. At Crowe International Schools, we inspire each child to achieve their God given individual personal excellence. As a result, they grow to become valuable members of our global community. Guided by the Nigerian Education Curriculum, we cater for children aged 18 months to young adults aged 18 years.

More About Us

Crowe Schools Core Values.

Where Excellence Meets Innovation: Crowe Schools, Where Discipline, Respect, Leadership, and Integrity Drive Success.

Academic Excellence

Crowe Schools strives for academic excellence, fostering a culture of high achievement and promoting intellectual growth among its students.

Discipline

The school emphasizes discipline as a core value, teaching students the importance of self-control, responsibility, and perseverance in their academic and personal pursuits.

Innovation

Crowe Schools values innovation and creativity, inspiring students to think critically, solve problems, and embrace new ideas to adapt to a rapidly changing world.

Integrity

The school upholds the values of honesty, integrity, and ethical conduct, instilling these principles in students to develop strong character and moral values.

Respect

Crowe Schools promotes a culture of respect, where students learn to appreciate and value diversity, treating others with kindness, empathy, and understanding.

Leardership

Crowe Schools nurtures leadership qualities in its students, empowering them to become responsible and visionary leaders who make positive contributions to society.
