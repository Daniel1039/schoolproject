Alh. S.O. Ogunremi

Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi, the esteemed proprietor of Crowe Schools, hails from the vibrant community of Iwo in Nigeria. His educational journey began at U.N.D Primary School in Oloola, Iwo, where he laid the foundation for his academic pursuits from 1962 to 1969. He continued to enrich his knowledge at an Arabic school until 1971. Taking his education further, Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi attended Baptist High School (BHS) in Adeeke, Iwo, completing his secondary education from 1972 to 1976. His dedication to learning led him to Oyo State College of Education in Ilesa, where he earned his NCE certificate in Biology and Chemistry Education between 1979 and 1982. Subsequently, he ventured into his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) at St. Monica Catholic School in Oturkpo, Benue State, Nigeria.

Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi's pursuit of academic excellence reached a pinnacle when he attended the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN) from 1986 to 1989, where he successfully obtained a B.Ed. in Biology Education. With a diverse background in management from various organizations across the nation, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role as the proprietor of Crowe Schools, ensuring the institution's strong commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. In recognition of his contributions, Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi was honored with the title of Alhaji in 2006.

Education

Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi's educational journey is a testament to his unwavering dedication to knowledge. Beginning at U.N.D Primary School in Oloola, Iwo, from 1962 to 1969, he built the foundation for his academic pursuits. His thirst for learning led him to an Arabic school from 1969 to 1971, expanding his horizons. Alhaji S.O. Ogunremi continued his educational odyssey at Baptist High School (BHS) in Adeeke, Iwo, completing his secondary education from 1972 to 1976. Further enriching his academic background, he attended Oyo State College of Education, Ilesa, from 1979 to 1982, earning his NCE certificate in Biology and Chemistry education. His journey culminated in obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Biology Education from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN), from 1986 to 1989. This unyielding commitment to lifelong learning and academic excellence underpins his prosperous entrepreneurial career and esteemed role as the Proprietor of Crowe Schools.
